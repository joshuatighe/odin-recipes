Brownies

Description

Brownies are baked squares of dense chocolate heaven. They come in various textures - fudgy, cakey, or chewy - depending on the recipe.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prep
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
  3. Combine Wet Ingredients
  4. Combine Bowls
  5. Bake
  6. Cool
